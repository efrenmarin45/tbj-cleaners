About Us

TBJ Janitorial has been servicing commercial and residential clients for over 10 years. We specialize in various industries, from servicing beauty salons and restaurants, to office spaces, schools and different size apartment complexes plus more. No job is too big or too small for our team. If you need any type of cleaning service, you can rely on our professional staff to get it done right the first time.


Our goal as a service provider is to clearly understand what our client’s needs are. We believe that by doing this, we can accomplish true customer satisfaction.


We also know the effort and dedication that goes into maintaining a business place and TBJ Janitorial makes sure it is reflected in the cleanliness of your area at first glance, that’s why our motto is, Relax! We’ve got it!
